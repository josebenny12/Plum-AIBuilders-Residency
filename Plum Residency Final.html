02 Who This Is For
Typical Background
  • Computer science graduate from a Tier-1 engineering college.
  • Have shipped real products to production.
  • Strong business acumen and problem-solving instincts.
  • Many residents are ex-founders or product builders.
  • People who understand how companies are actually built.
  • You can read a codebase and a P&L.
The Standard
  • You have something concrete to show. Not a title. Actual outcomes with numbers.
  • Deep technical ability with sharp business thinking.
  • You know how to build — and understand why it matters.
  • You move fast without breaking things that matter. You know the difference.
  • You have strong opinions on product, and can defend them with data.
  • You are comfortable with ambiguity. You don't need a spec to start.
A feature that doesn't move a metric is a liability.
A beautiful system no one uses is an indulgence.
If that feels obvious to you, you belong here.
